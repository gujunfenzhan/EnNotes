同位语

同位语

(相同+地位+成分)

例子:My mom,Shufen is the authority of the family.

概念/作用:n2解释n1，且n2=n1

I like the XH,a girl with a big head.

a girl with a big head解释XH

形式:

N/引导词+句子(完整句) = 同位语从句

The transportation tool,(a bus/a subway(同位语)), is convenient.

定语从句or同位语从句?

区分定语从句和同位语从句

The information (that he heard) is true.不完整，他听到?，缺成分

The information (that he did) is true.完整，他死了这个消息，与information同地位

识别+处理:

  • 识别
n n/,n/:/---/()

We customers are smart.(n n)

customers 解释 we

Xh,a beautiful girl is smart.(,n)

  • 处理

括起来+这个/这些/也就是
