定语

定语

作用

让主干更完整

与n相关的:定语/同位语

与动作相关的:状语

概念/作用:

修饰n:让n更加生动

限定n范围

  • 限定词+adj+n
    • 冠词:a/an/the
    • XQ is a dead person.
    • 数量词:one、two、three.../many= (a + n + of/ns+of)
    • a number of
    • a lot of
    • an amount of
    • a variety of
    • a sea of
    • a mountain of
    • a train of coal
    • lots of
  • 后置定语
    • adj+介+n (形容词短语)
    • XQ is a dead person away from us.
    • 介+n (介词短语)
    • XQ is a dead person on a bed.
    • 分词
      • doing sth.<现在分词>
      • XQ is a dead person holding a death certificate.
      • People loving you like every aspect of you.
      • a dog barking to us.
      • ved/ven+by+n <过去分词>
      • XQ is a dead person surrounded by his families.
      • People loved by you are happy.
      • a dog abandoned by us.
    • 不定式作定语
      • n to do sth (n = to do sth)
      • I have a plan to buy house.(plan=to buy house这个计划)
      • A habit to read book.
      • A dream to go to BJ university.
      • A hobby to go shopping.
      • n to do sth (动作由前面名词所发生)
      • I have a friend to have 5 million.(动作由朋友发出)
      • n to do (名词是后面动作的承受对象)
      • I have a letter to write.(letter是write动作的承受对象)

识别+处理

识别:位置+形式

(左二右五 名词的右边但凡不是谓语，都是修饰)

处理:括起来 n(...下一个n) 然后换顺序换到n前
