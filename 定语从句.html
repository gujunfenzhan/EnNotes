定语从句

定语从句

  • 定语从句=引导词+句子
    • 例:I know the boy (定语从句 who stands under the tree(定语 besides the road))

来源

  • 合并两个具有相同名词的句子，第二相同名词换成连词
    • 例1:I know the boy. He stands under the tree.
    • I know the boy who stands under the tree.
    • 例2:I know the boy, He smiles to us.
    • I know the boy who smiles to us.
    • 例3:I know the boy. We saw him yesterday.
    • I know the boy who we saw yesterday.

作用

  • 修饰限定名词(限定性定语从句):n+引导词+句子
    • I like the girl who has a big head.
    • 我喜欢那个大头的女孩
  • 补充说明名词(非限定性定语从句):n,引导词+句子
    • I like the girl,who has a big head.
    • 补充说明这个女孩，但不重要
  • 表示原因
    • I like the girl,who has a big head.
    • 我喜欢一个女孩，因为他有大头

连接词

  • 连接代词(who/whom/which/that)+不完整句
    • I remember the town. I visited it last year.
    • I remember the town which/that(连接代词) i visited last year.
  • 介词+连接代词+完整句 = 连接副词(when/where/why)+完整句
    • I remember the town. In the town, i was born.
    • I remember the town, in which(=where) i was born.
    • I am standing on a bridge,Under the bridge there is a dog.
    • I am standing on a bridge, under which/where there is a dog.
    • under为介词，连接代词需要放在介词后面
  • 连接形容词(whose)+完整句
    • I remember the house. Its roof is white.
    • I remember the house whose roof is white.
  • as 引出非限定性定语从句
    • She is smart,as we know.
    • 他很聪明,正如我们所见.
    • 正如...
    • As the time goes by(时间状语从句),we have grown up.
    • such as引出定语从句:这样一个...的n
      • He is such a boy as we like.
      • 他是这样一个我们所喜欢的男孩

识别+处理

  • 识别方式
    • N/,+引导词+句子
  • 处理方式
    • 括起来 n+引导词...(引导词/谓语v前/句末)前，然后独立成句
